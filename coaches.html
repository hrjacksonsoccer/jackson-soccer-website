Youth Trainer Pass

The NJYS Youth Trainer program identifies individuals who are reliable, interested in helping children, and want to serve as Youth Trainers assisting adult coaches during practices and games.

  • Youth Trainers must be 18 or older to hold a USYS Coach's Pass issued by NJYS.
  • Must complete a medical waiver certification.
  • Must maintain a copy of their NJYS State Certificate and CDC Concussion Awareness Certification.
  • An adult coach must always be supervising and leading during any training event.

Coaching Resources

JSC provides support for all volunteer coaches. Here are tools and contacts to help you run a successful season.

Recreational Coaches

  • Peanuts, Mini Kickers, and Micro Divisions have JSC trainer-led sessions.
  • Collegiate & Major League coaches receive trainer-led Wednesday night practice support.
  • No prior soccer experience required — we train and support all volunteer coaches.
  • Coaches will receive team rosters and schedule details 1–2 weeks before the season.

Travel Coaches

  • Contact the Overall Commissioner (Ryan Monday) for field scheduling and travel league info.
  • JSC supports 30+ travel teams each Fall and Spring season.
  • Leagues include EDP, MAPS, JAGS, MOSA, and Ocean County Recreation.
💡 Want to volunteer? We need coaches at every level. No experience necessary — just energy and a willingness to help kids enjoy soccer. Email us to get started.
