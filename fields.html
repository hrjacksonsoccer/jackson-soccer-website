Field Rental

The Jackson Mills Vista fields are designated for soccer and may only be used for soccer games and soccer activities.

Scheduling is based on availability. Priority scheduling is given to Jackson Soccer Club first, then Youth Sports Organizations (non-profit). Priority is also given to requests for official games first (both teams in uniform and referee assigned), then scrimmages, and then practices.

Permits will be issued for hours between sunrise and sunset.

📧 For field rental inquiries, contact: info@jacksonsoccer.com
