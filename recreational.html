About the Program

The Jackson Soccer Club Intramurals is a recreational program open to children ranging in age from 3 to 17 years old. The program strives to create a safe, fun, and exciting environment for children to learn the game of soccer.

We form teams and divisions based on age. We follow US Soccer principles to develop kids — physically, technically, strategically, and grow their Soccer IQ.

We offer both a Fall and Spring season:

  • Fall: Teams begin practicing mid-August; games run September to early November.
  • Spring: Teams begin practicing end of March; games run April to mid-June.
🙋 We need volunteer coaches at every level — no experience required!
We have trainers who support coaches throughout the season. If you have energy and want to help, we want you.

Important Information

Team Placement

  • Teams are chosen at random based on age/grade level/gender.
  • We cannot accept player, coach, or teammate requests.
  • We do our best to make teams as fair as possible.
  • Coaches will reach out to families 1–2 weeks before the season starts.

Register Early

⚠️ Please register early. Late registrations cannot be guaranteed team placement or uniforms. Full payment is required to be placed on a waitlist.

Waitlist Policy

We run a waitlist when registration approaches our limit for any division. Registering for the waitlist holds your place in line. If you are on the waitlist and we don't assign you to a team by Week 3, you can receive a full refund.

If a division needs coaches, players whose parent volunteers to coach will be given priority on the waitlist.

Emails & Communication

Make sure you have not blocked Jackson Soccer Club so you can receive emails about the season. We will email everything you need to know before the season begins.
